# Weddings

Plan and celebrate beautiful Catholic wedding ceremonies with Outward Sign.

## Overview

The Weddings module helps you manage every aspect of a Catholic wedding ceremony, from initial planning through the ceremony itself.

## Creating a Wedding

### Step 1: Basic Information

1. Navigate to **Weddings** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Wedding**
3. Enter the following information:
   - **Bride** - Select or create a person record
   - **Groom** - Select or create a person record
   - **Status** - Active, Inactive, or Completed
   - **Language** - English, Spanish, or Latin
   - **Notes** - Internal notes for staff

### Step 2: Event Details

Add up to four events for a complete wedding celebration:

- **Wedding Ceremony** - The marriage liturgy (required)
- **Wedding Reception** - Post-ceremony celebration
- **Wedding Rehearsal** - Practice ceremony
- **Rehearsal Dinner** - Pre-wedding meal

For each event, specify:
- Date and time
- Location
- Expected attendees

### Step 3: Witnesses and Family

- **Best Man** - Primary male witness
- **Maid/Matron of Honor** - Primary female witness
- **Witnesses** - Additional official witnesses
- **Parents** - Parents of bride and groom

### Step 4: Liturgy Planning

Choose your readings:

1. **First Reading** - Old Testament reading
2. **Responsorial Psalm** - Sung or spoken response
3. **Second Reading** - New Testament reading
4. **Gospel** - Gospel acclamation and reading

Add custom petitions:
- Use a template or write custom prayers
- Edit the Prayer of the Faithful
- Add special intentions

### Step 5: Ministers

Assign liturgical roles:

- **Presider** - Priest or deacon celebrating the marriage
- **Lectors** - Readers for the liturgy
- **Musicians** - Cantor, organist, choir
- **Altar Servers** - Assistants to the presider

## Wedding Templates

Outward Sign includes built-in wedding templates:

- **Full Ceremony Script (English)** - Complete wedding liturgy in English
- **Full Ceremony Script (Spanish)** - Complete wedding liturgy in Spanish

Templates include:
- Entrance procession
- Liturgy of the Word
- Rite of Marriage
- Liturgy of the Eucharist (if applicable)
- Concluding rites

## Printing Your Wedding Script

1. Open the wedding detail page
2. Click **Print View** or **Download PDF**
3. Review the generated script
4. Print or save for the presider

The script includes:
- Complete order of ceremony
- All readings with citations
- Petitions and responses
- Minister assignments
- Custom notes for the presider

## Wedding Workflow

### Initial Contact
1. Couple contacts parish
2. Create wedding record with basic info
3. Schedule initial meeting

### Planning Phase
1. Add all event details
2. Select readings and music
3. Schedule rehearsal
4. Confirm ministers

### Before the Wedding
1. Print ceremony script
2. Confirm all participants
3. Hold rehearsal
4. Final review with presider

### After the Wedding
1. Update status to "Completed"
2. Archive ceremony script
3. Send thank you notes
4. File marriage certificate

## Tips for Beautiful Weddings

- **Start Early** - Begin planning at least 3 months ahead
- **Use Templates** - Customize the built-in templates to save time
- **Communicate Clearly** - Share event details with all participants
- **Print Early** - Have scripts ready 48 hours before the ceremony
- **Be Flexible** - Work with couples to personalize their celebration

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can we have a wedding without a full Mass?**
A: Yes, you can celebrate the Rite of Marriage within or outside of Mass.

**Q: How many readings are required?**
A: Typically 2-3 readings plus a Gospel, but consult your presider.

**Q: Can we write our own vows?**
A: Couples can add personal statements, but the required vows are fixed.

**Q: Who can be a witness?**
A: Any Catholic in good standing, typically 16 years or older.
