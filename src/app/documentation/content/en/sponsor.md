# Sponsor Outward Sign

## Supporting the Beauty of the Liturgy for the Church and the World

Outward Sign exists to help Catholic parishes around the world celebrate the sacraments with the beauty, dignity, and preparation they deserve. As a free, open-source tool, we rely on the generosity of sponsors who share our vision of making excellent sacrament preparation accessible to every parish, regardless of budget.

Your sponsorship directly supports the development, maintenance, and growth of Outward Sign, enabling parishes worldwide to:

- **Plan and celebrate beautiful liturgies** with proper preparation and dignity
- **Collaborate effectively** between priests, staff, and families
- **Print professional liturgical scripts** for presiders
- **Manage sacramental records** with ease and security
- **Serve multilingual communities** with bilingual English-Spanish support

---

## Why Sponsor Outward Sign?

### For the Church

Every sponsorship dollar helps build better tools for parishes to fulfill their sacred mission. When parishes can celebrate the sacraments beautifully, the entire Church benefits:

- **Evangelization through Beauty** - Well-prepared liturgies draw people closer to Christ
- **Parish Efficiency** - Staff spend less time on logistics and more time on pastoral care
- **Global Impact** - Free access means parishes worldwide, including those with limited resources, benefit equally
- **Open Source** - Transparent development ensures the tool serves the Church's needs

### For the World

The sacraments are not just internal Church events‚Äîthey are powerful signs of God's grace to the world:

- **Witness to Excellence** - Beautiful celebrations demonstrate that the Church values what is sacred
- **Cultural Impact** - Weddings, baptisms, and funerals touch the wider community
- **Missionary Activity** - Accessible tools help mission parishes in developing nations
- **Technological Evangelization** - Modern tools meet people where they are

## Sponsorship FAQ

### Is my sponsorship tax-deductible?

Outward Sign is a project of CatholicOS and Lolek Productions. We are working on 501(c)(3) status to make all sponsorships tax-deductible. Please contact us for current tax information.

### Can I sponsor anonymously?

Yes. While we'd love to recognize your generosity, we respect sponsors who wish to remain anonymous.

### Can I sponsor in honor or memory of someone?

Absolutely. We can include memorial or honorific dedications with your sponsorship recognition.

### What if our parish can't afford sponsorship?

Outward Sign is a **free tool** for all parishes. Sponsorship is never required to use the full application. Wealthy parishes and generous donors sponsor the project so that every parish, regardless of resources, can benefit.

### Can we sponsor specific features?

Yes! Cathedral and Guardian sponsors can work with us to prioritize features that benefit their communities and the wider Church.

### How often should we renew sponsorship?

Most sponsors commit annually, but we welcome multi-year commitments, quarterly giving, or one-time gifts. Whatever works best for your budget.

### Can dioceses sponsor for multiple parishes?

Yes! We offer diocesan sponsorship packages that cover multiple parishes. Contact us to discuss.
