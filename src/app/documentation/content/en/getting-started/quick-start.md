# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Outward Sign in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [outwardsign.church](https://outwardsign.church)
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Check your email for a verification link

## Step 2: Set Up Your Parish

After logging in for the first time, you'll be guided through the onboarding process:

1. **Parish Information** - Enter your parish name, address, and contact details
2. **Parish Settings** - Configure default language, timezone, and preferences
3. **Invite Staff** - Add other staff members to collaborate

## Step 3: Add Locations

Before creating events, add your parish locations:

1. Navigate to **Locations** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Location**
3. Add your church, chapel, parish hall, etc.

## Step 4: Add People

Build your parish directory:

1. Navigate to **People** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Person**
3. Add parishioners, staff members, and ministers

## Step 5: Plan Your First Sacramental Celebration

Now you're ready to plan a sacramental celebration:

1. Navigate to the appropriate module in the sidebar (**Weddings**, **Funerals**, **Baptisms**, etc.)
2. Click **Create** to start a new record
3. Fill in the participant information (bride/groom, deceased, parents, etc.)
4. Link the celebration to an event (or create a new calendar event)
5. Choose readings and customize petitions
6. Assign ministers and liturgical roles
7. Save and print your liturgical script

**Note:** Events (found in the Calendar) are standalone calendar entries for scheduling. Liturgical planning, minister assignments, and script generation happen in the specific sacrament modules.

## Tips for Success

- **Start Small** - Begin with one or two upcoming events
- **Invite Your Team** - Collaboration makes planning easier
- **Explore Templates** - Use built-in petition and liturgy templates
- **Print Early** - Test your printed scripts before the celebration
