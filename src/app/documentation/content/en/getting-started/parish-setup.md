# Parish Setup

Learn how to configure your parish settings in Outward Sign.

## Accessing Parish Settings

1. Click your profile icon in the top right corner
2. Select **Parish Settings** from the dropdown menu

**Note:** Only users with the **Admin** role can access parish settings.

## Parish Information

### Basic Information

Configure your parish's core details:

- **Parish Name** - Your parish's official name
- **Address** - Street address, city, state/province, postal code, country
- **Contact Information** - Phone number and email address
- **Website** - Your parish website URL (optional)

### Default Settings

- **Default Language** - Choose English or Spanish for default display
- **Timezone** - Set your parish's timezone for correct event scheduling

## Team Management

Manage your parish team members and roles. See the [Inviting Team Members](../user-guides/inviting-staff) guide for detailed instructions.

### Viewing Team Members

In the Team Management section, you'll see:
- All current parish members
- Their roles (Admin, Staff, Ministry-Leader, Parishioner)
- Module access (for Ministry-Leaders)
- When they joined the parish

### Inviting New Members

1. Enter their email address
2. Select their role
3. If Ministry-Leader, select which modules they can access
4. Click **Send Invitation**

See [Inviting Team Members](../user-guides/inviting-staff) for complete details.

### Managing Pending Invitations

View all pending invitations and:
- **Resend** - Send a new invitation email
- **Revoke** - Cancel the invitation

## Templates

**Admin-only feature**

Create and manage custom templates for:
- Petitions (Prayer of the Faithful)
- Liturgical scripts
- Ceremony outlines

Templates help standardize your parish's liturgical celebrations while allowing for customization when needed.

## Best Practices

### Keep Information Current

- Update contact information promptly
- Review team member access quarterly
- Archive inactive team members

### Security

- Only grant Admin access to trusted staff
- Use Ministry-Leader role for specialized coordinators
- Regularly review pending invitations

### Communication

- Ensure all team members know their roles
- Provide training for new staff
- Keep a backup list of all team member emails
