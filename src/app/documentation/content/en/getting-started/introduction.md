# Introduction to Outward Sign

Welcome to **Outward Sign**, the sacrament and sacramental management tool designed specifically for Catholic parishes.

## What is Outward Sign?

Outward Sign helps parish staff plan, communicate, and celebrate the sacraments and sacramentals that are at the heart of Catholic parish life. Our belief is simple: **The Sacraments are the core activity of the Catholic Parish**, and their proper celebration at every step is the evangelizing work of parishes.

## Core Features

- **Shared Preparation** - Collaborate with presiders, staff, and families
- **Event Calendar** - Organize all sacramental events in one place
- **Print & Export** - Generate readings, scripts, and liturgical documents
- **Language Management** - Support for English and Spanish liturgies
- **People Management** - Maintain your parish directory and role assignments

## Who Uses Outward Sign?

Outward Sign is designed for three main user roles:

### Admins
Parish administrators who manage settings, invite staff, and oversee the entire system.

### Staff
Parish staff members (priests, deacons, liturgical coordinators) who plan and execute sacramental celebrations.

### Parishioners
Families and individuals participating in sacraments who can view and share their event details.
