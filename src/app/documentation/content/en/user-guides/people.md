# Managing People

Learn how to manage your parish directory and people records in Outward Sign.

## Overview

The People module helps you maintain a directory of everyone involved in your parish's sacramental life, including:
- Parishioners
- Family members
- Ministers (lectors, servers, musicians)
- Presiders (priests, deacons)
- Staff members

## Viewing People

1. Navigate to **People** in the sidebar
2. Browse or search for specific people
3. Click on a person's name to view their full profile

### Search and Filters

- **Search by name** - Type a person's name in the search box
- **Filter by role** - View only ministers, presiders, or specific groups
- **Export directory** - Download your parish directory as CSV

## Creating a New Person

1. Navigate to **People**
2. Click **Create Person**
3. Fill in the person's information:
   - **Full Name** - First, middle, and last name
   - **Email** - Contact email address
   - **Phone** - Primary phone number
   - **Address** - Street address, city, state, postal code
   - **Date of Birth** - Optional, helpful for sacramental records
   - **Notes** - Any additional information

4. Click **Save**

## Editing a Person

1. Find the person in the People list
2. Click on their name to view their profile
3. Click **Edit**
4. Update their information
5. Click **Save**

## Person Roles and Assignments

### Assigning Liturgical Roles

When planning sacramental celebrations, you can assign people to specific roles:

- **Presiders** - Mark priests and deacons who can preside at celebrations
- **Lectors** - Readers for the liturgy
- **Musicians** - Cantors, organists, choir members
- **Servers** - Altar servers
- **Ministers** - Extraordinary ministers of Holy Communion

### Groups

People can belong to parish groups (e.g., choir, funeral ministry, wedding coordinators). See the Groups module for more details.

## Person Profile Details

Each person's profile shows:

### Contact Information
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical address

### Sacramental History
View all sacramental celebrations this person has participated in:
- As a primary participant (bride, groom, deceased, baptized)
- As a minister (presider, lector, musician)
- As a witness or family member

### Groups and Ministries
- All groups they belong to
- Ministry roles they serve in
- Availability and scheduling preferences

## Privacy and Sharing

### Who Can See People Records?

- **Admins** - Can view and edit all people
- **Staff** - Can view and edit all people
- **Ministry-Leaders** - Can view people relevant to their modules
- **Parishioners** - Can only view people connected to their own sacramental records

### Sharing with Families

When planning sacramental celebrations, you can share event details with family members:

1. Create the sacramental record (wedding, baptism, etc.)
2. Share it with the family
3. They can view (but not edit) the celebration details

## Best Practices

### Data Entry

- **Be consistent** - Use consistent naming and formatting
- **Keep it current** - Update contact information promptly
- **Add notes** - Include helpful context (e.g., "prefers emails," "Spanish speaker")
- **Verify information** - Double-check spellings, especially for sacramental certificates

### Privacy

- **Limit access** - Only share information when necessary
- **Respect preferences** - Honor communication preferences
- **Secure data** - Don't export or share the directory externally without permission

### Directory Maintenance

- **Regular updates** - Review and update the directory quarterly
- **Remove duplicates** - Merge duplicate person records
- **Archive inactive** - Keep the active directory focused on current parishioners

## Common Tasks

### Adding a Family for a Wedding

1. Create person record for the **Bride**
2. Create person record for the **Groom**
3. When creating the wedding, select both from the people picker
4. Add parents and witnesses as needed

### Setting Up a Minister Schedule

1. Navigate to **People**
2. Find the minister's profile
3. Note their contact information and availability
4. When planning celebrations, assign them to appropriate roles

### Exporting the Directory

1. Navigate to **People**
2. Click **Export** or the download icon
3. Select **CSV format**
4. Open in Excel or Google Sheets for mailing labels, phone trees, etc.
