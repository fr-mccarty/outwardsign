## Tips for Getting Help

To help us assist you quickly:

1. **Be as specific as possible** - Include details about what you're trying to do and what's happening
2. **Include screenshots** - A picture is worth a thousand words! Screenshots help us understand the issue quickly
3. **Describe the steps** - What were you doing when the issue occurred?

The more details you provide, the faster we can help resolve your issue.

## Response Time

Our team is small but passionate about serving the Church. **We aim to respond within 48 hours.**

We appreciate your patience and are committed to helping you make the most of Outward Sign for your parish's sacramental celebrations.
